What is creator protection?

Creator Protection is a spam filter designed to block harmful messages and comments in streams and videos. During a stream, CP will scan messages being sent in the chat, looking for anything offensive, inappropriate, violent, or slanderous, while also allowing regular, harmless messages to stay. Creator Protection will enable creators to have control over what is being said in their stream, creating a less toxic and more family-friendly environment.
